Photography

My photography can be found at Cheddarness.com. I used to ask my siblings and parents for their old film cameras, since it would give me an excuse to go out and explore the world. While I do not do too much film photography now (because the film and processing of the film has gotten very expesnive), I spent a considerable amount of time exploring the world through my photography. I eventually displayed my photos in a few exhibitions across Los Angeles.
